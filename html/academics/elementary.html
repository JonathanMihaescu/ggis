Elementary

Title 1

Our elementary program aims to build a foundation for each student to become lifelong learners. We accomplish this by focusing on Mathematics and Literacy during the elementary years. In addition to these core classes, the students have interactive Science and Social studies classes multiple times each week. To help students improve with their English skills, we have vocabulary, spelling, and handwriting classes in every elementary class. The elementary classes also have music, art, and gym classes that are taught by specialized teachers. All this is done with the goal of promoting an expansive elementary experience that allows students to find areas in education that they are interested in. As they continue in our school, students are encouraged to continue to explore areas of interest that have been developed in their elementary years.
